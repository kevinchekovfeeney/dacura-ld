Abstract

The political violence (PV) ontology is designed to describe historical events of political violence and reports of those events.

Introduction

This ontology will be used to describe the US political violence data set collected by Prof. Peter Turchin and the UK and Ireland political violence dataset collected by the Knowledge and Data Engineering Group at Trinity College Dublin. The major concepts modelled in the ontology are (historical) Events, real or imagined, Reports of Events and Political Violence events.
